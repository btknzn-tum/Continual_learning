SETUP
  Task-incremental CL (task id known at test). Frozen backbone (ResNet-50
  ImageNet), features cached per depth (layer1..layer4; multi-tap = z-scored
  concat). Trainable: shared 2-layer MLP adapter (d_hidden=256) + per-task
  linear heads. Protection = quadratic importance penalty (MAS/EWC/SI) on
  adapter weights. All methods share the SAME trainer, epochs, and
  validation-based HP tuning (sweep seed 7, disjoint from reporting seeds).
  Reporting seeds: 42,123,456,789,1337 (n=seeds completed so far).
  Metrics: AvgAcc% (mean final acc over tasks), F=Forgetting%, P=Plasticity%.
